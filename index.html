What is google

Google is a multinational technology company primarily known for its search engine, which processes billions of queries every day. Besides search, Google offers a wide range of services and products, including Gmail, Google Maps, YouTube, Google Drive, and Google Docs. It's also the creator of Android, the world's most widely used mobile operating system. As one of the largest tech companies, Google has become a major player in internet advertising, cloud computing, and artificial intelligence.

myhumber

MyHumber is the student portal for Humber College, designed to help students manage their academic journey. Through MyHumber, students can register for courses, view grades, manage finances, and access key academic resources. It serves as the main gateway for communications between students and the administration. Additionally, the portal provides students with access to support services, such as career counseling and academic advising.

What is Toronto Metropolitan University?

Toronto Metropolitan University (formerly Ryerson University) is a leading institution in downtown Toronto, focused on career-oriented education and applied research. The university is well-known for programs in business, engineering, arts, media, and design. It encourages students to think critically and develop entrepreneurial skills, preparing them for real-world challenges. With its urban location and emphasis on innovation, Toronto Metropolitan University plays a key role in shaping the future of industries and technologies.
